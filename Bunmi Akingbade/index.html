About Me

Meet Bunmi Akingbade

Bunmi Akingbade is a Human Resource, Financial, and Business Consultant with over two decades of corporate experience in the Nigerian banking sector. A former Regional Operations Manager and Branch Manager at Stanbic IBTC Bank, she brings practical expertise in business development, sales and marketing, relationship management, human resource management, operations, and risk management.

Through Lacare Staffing Solutions, Bunmi provides recruitment, training, and consulting services with a vision to help build one of Africa's leading HR companies. She is also a mentor, coach, inspirational speaker, and author committed to productivity, personal development, leadership, and self-empowerment.
